CodaBox

Import CODA automatically

Connect your bank accounts to CodaBox to automatically retrieve your CODA bank statements.

User guide

Request a token from CodaBox

  1. Go to Accounting > Configuration > CodaBox Settings
  2. Press on REQUEST TOKEN to request a token from CodaBox
  3. In the wizard, enter your VAT number and press on REQUEST TOKEN
  4. An email with the token will be sent to the email address you provided when contracting with CodaBox

Get your CodaBox credentials

  1. Once you have received the one-time token from CodaBox, go to Accounting > Configuration > CodaBox Settings
  2. Press on RETRIEVE CREDENTIALS
  3. In the wizard, enter the one time token received on the CodaBox contact email address then press on RETRIEVE CREDENTIALS
  4. CodaBox username and password will now be filled and Odoo will now automatically retrieve Coda files from your CodaBox connected bank accounts
